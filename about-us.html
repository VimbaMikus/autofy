What is Autofy?

"Autofy" is Riga Technical University Computer Science and Information Technology Faculty one group project, whose task was to develop a multi-criteria decision-making application for car purchasing.

What is Project Vision?

For car buyer who wants to buy a car the car comparison tool is a web page that helps buyer find the right car more efficiently. Unlike used car lot our solution helps the user choose a car from home.

Why choose Autofy?

Car find and compare options

We offer you a great opportunity to choose the right car with the multi-criteria search tool, but choosing a car is a difficult process, so we also offer you a car comparison tool with its perfectly working and specially developed for this purpose comparison algorithm.

Good offers for official car dealers

In addition, we offer authorized car dealers to place their advertising banners in the footer of our site, and all cars available on our site can be viewed for more information about the car on official dealers' websites by clicking on "Details" button.

Option for users

There is an additional option on our site for the user to add your own car so that it can be compared to other available cars.
